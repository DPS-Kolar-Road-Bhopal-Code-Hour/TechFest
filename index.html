Welcome to Les Voyages progressifs!

We are a hosting service available worldwide. No matter where you go, we can provide you with a range of accommodations to suit your needs. Besides that, our corporate goal is to spur progress in underprivileged communities and areas; therefore, more than 80% of our profits go towards providing education and internet access to impoverished communities.

Learn More

Change the world while having fun!

As aforementioned, we intend to utilize more than 80% of our earnings to sponsor impoverished communities. Thus far, we have been able to assist disenfranchised communities in the sub-Saharan region get access to the internet by getting them a stable internet connection and medium-end computers. The internet is a crucial resource that can help spur education in said regions. You can be a part of our mission by simply booking a fun vacation to the destination of your dreams!
