Covid Safety Guidelines

Mask Mandates

The staff of the hotel wears masks and follow all the necessary norms to prevent the spread of air borne diseases. They follow social distancing. It is mandatory to wear masks in most countries, for more information, please contact the particular hotel you are going to stay in.

Vaccine Mandates

All the staff has taken all the doses of vaccine for COVID-19. We, the hotel management and staff, are doing everything we can to ensure that nothing goes wrong. To live in the hotels, you need to show the COVID-19 Vaccine certification to gain entry.

Regular Sanitization

The staff does regular sanitization and after every contact with a person, sanitizes hand to reduce transmission of germs from one person to another. There is always complete hygienic condition in the premises.
