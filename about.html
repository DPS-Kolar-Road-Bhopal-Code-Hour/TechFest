About Us

Les Voyages progressifs is an organization that aims to provide an exquisite and diverse travel experience, all while helping impoverished people. We have hotels in 18 locations in 11 countries: The United States Of America, Canada, Ireland, The United Kingdom, France, Germany, Norway, Australia, Japan, South Korea, and China. Some of these hotels—namely New York, Las Vegas, San Francisco, and Toronto—have an exclusive amenity - a sports facility. The facility comes included in the luxurious package without an extra charge. There are multiple packages for you to choose from: a basic package, A premium package, and a luxurious package. The basic package includes the following: A 2,000 square feet living area that includes a bedroom, a spacious living room, a dining room, an entertainment room, and two bathrooms, all at just $100 per week. The premium package entails the following: a 3,000 square feet living area that includes two bedrooms, a spacious living room, a dining room, an entertainment room, two bathrooms, and access to the pool, all at just $180 per week. The luxurious package entails the following: everything that the premium package has, as well as access to extra amenities such as the sports facility(in limited regions), a taxi service, helicopter rides, and city tour services, all at just $300 per week. We have pledged to spend more than 80% of our profits(after employee salaries) to help impoverished areas. Check out the home page to get more details on donations.
